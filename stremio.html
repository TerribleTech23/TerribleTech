Stremio + Real-Debrid

The core setup for a high-speed, buffer-free media experience. By offloading stream fetching to a Debrid service, you eliminate ISP throttling and peer-dependency.

Pro Tip: Using a Debrid service encrypted via HTTPS means you don't technically need a VPN for privacy while streaming, though it's always recommended for overall security.
Step 01

Acquire Real-Debrid

Real-Debrid acts as your personal "downloader" in the cloud. It fetches torrents on high-speed servers and serves them to you as a direct download.

Get Real-Debrid
Screen: Real-Debrid Dashboard & API Token Section
Step 02

Configure Torrentio

Torrentio is the bridge between Stremio and Real-Debrid. You must configure it in a web browser before it appears in your app.

  1. Visit the Torrentio configuration page.
  2. In the "Debrid Provider" dropdown, select Real-Debrid.
  3. Paste your API Token from Step 1 into the field.
  4. Click "Install" to open the link in your Stremio app.
Configure Torrentio
Step 03

Add Media Scrapers (Optional)

For even better link discovery, you can add Comet or Debridio. These work similarly to Torrentio and provide backup links if one service is down.

Screen: Stremio Add-ons List showing installed scrapers
Step 04

Optimize Your Device

On Android (S25 Ultra) or Android TV (Onn 4K), ensure the following settings are enabled in Stremio:

  • Hardware Acceleration: ON
  • Torrent Profile: Ultra Fast (doesn't matter much for Debrid, but good for local)
  • Frame Rate Matching: Enabled (for smooth TV playback)
