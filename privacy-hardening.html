Digital Hardening 101

Mastering your data footprint by securing your communications and network traffic.

1. Encrypted Communication

Move away from data-mining platforms and secure your messages and metadata at the source.

  • Proton Mail: End-to-end encrypted email based in Switzerland.
  • Signal: The gold standard for private messaging. No ads, no trackers, and no data collection.
  • Alias Management: Use Proton Pass or SimpleLogin to create unique aliases, keeping your primary email hidden from data brokers.

2. Network Privacy & Ad Blocking

Your ISP shouldn't see your activity. Mask your traffic and kill trackers before they even load.

  • Proton VPN: Encrypt your entire internet connection and bypass censorship.
  • NetShield Ad-blocker: Use Proton VPN’s built-in DNS filtering to block ads, malware, and trackers at the network level.
  • Private DNS: Configure your device to use encrypted DNS providers for an extra layer of stealth.

3. Account Security

Passwords aren't enough. Secure your logins with hardware-level or open-source authentication.

  • MFA with Aegis: An open-source 2FA app for Android that keeps your tokens encrypted and offline.
  • Recovery: Always store your backup codes in an encrypted vault or offline physical storage.
